Introduction

FontsReviewer ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address (for authentication)
  • Nickname (display name)
  • Password (encrypted and stored securely via Supabase)

Usage Data

  • Fountain reviews and ratings you submit
  • Your statistics (total reviews, average score)
  • Your best-rated fountain

Location Data

  • Coarse location (approximate, ~100m accuracy) - ONLY when you open the map
  • Used to display your position on the map
  • Used to enforce 300m radius restriction for Operator role users
  • We NEVER track or store your location history
  • Location data is NOT sent to our servers
  • Location is only used locally on your device

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device type and Android version (for crash reporting via Google Play)
  • App usage statistics (anonymous, via Google Play Console)

How We Use Your Information

  • Authentication: To create and manage your account
  • Core Features: To save your reviews, calculate stats, and display leaderboard
  • Map Display: To show your current location on the map (not stored)
  • Distance Enforcement: To verify Operator users are within 300m of fountains (not stored)
  • Crash Reporting: To identify and fix bugs (anonymous)

Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored securely on Supabase (PostgreSQL) with encryption in transit (HTTPS)
  • Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
  • We use Row Level Security (RLS) policies to protect your data
  • Your location is NEVER sent to servers or stored anywhere

Data Sharing

We DO NOT sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Supabase: Our backend provider (privacy policy)
  • Google Play Services: For app distribution and crash reporting
  • Mapbox: For map display (privacy policy)

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data at any time in the app
  • Edit your nickname and reviews
  • Delete your account and all associated data (via Profile settings)

When you delete your account:

  • Your email, nickname, and all reviews are permanently deleted
  • This action cannot be undone
  • Your account is deleted within 24 hours

Anonymous Usage

You can browse fountains and read reviews without creating an account.

No data is collected during anonymous browsing except:

  • Location permission (to show your position on map, if granted)
  • Anonymous crash reports (if app crashes)

Children's Privacy

FontsReviewer is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Showing an in-app notification for major changes

Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data retained as long as your account exists
  • Deleted accounts: Data permanently deleted within 24 hours
  • Anonymous crash logs: Retained for 90 days

Third-Party Services

Our app uses these third-party services:

  • Supabase (authentication & database): https://supabase.com/privacy
  • Mapbox (maps): https://www.mapbox.com/legal/privacy
  • Google Play Services (location): https://policies.google.com/privacy

Legal Basis (GDPR)

For users in the EU, our legal basis for processing your data:

  • Consent: You provide consent when creating an account
  • Legitimate Interest: Crash reporting and app improvements
  • Contract: Processing necessary to provide app features

Your GDPR Rights (EU Users)

If you're in the EU, you have additional rights:

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to rectification (edit your data)
  • Right to erasure (delete your account)
  • Right to data portability (export your data - contact us)
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time

FontsReviewer is built with privacy in mind. We only collect what's necessary to provide the service.
